Banana Bread

Banana bread is a moist and flavorful baked bread-like treat made primarily from ripe bananas. It is a popular and comforting baked good known for its sweet, slightly tangy taste and tender texture.

Required Ingredients

  • 2-3 Ripe Bananas
  • 1 1/3 Cup Butter, Salted or Unsalted
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Pinch of Salt
  • 3/4 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour

Steps to Making Delicious Homemade Banana Bread

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Mash the bananas and add the melted butter.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt.
  4. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  5. Mix in the flour.
  6. One mixture is smooth, pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 55-65 minutes or until a wooden toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool and enjoy!
